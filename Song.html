Paramore

Still Into You

"Still Into You" by Paramore is an upbeat pop-rock song about enduring love and
infatuation despite time and challenges. Released as a single from their 2013 album
Paramore, the song expresses a deep connection between two people
who remain drawn to each other. Its catchy melody and energetic rhythm convey the thrill of lasting love.
With powerful vocals from Hayley Williams, the song captures the intensity of affection
that persists over time. "Still Into You" celebrates a strong emotional bond, showing
that some connections stay strong despite life's trials.

Produced by Justin Meldal-Johnsen and Hayley Williams
Release Date March 14,2013
